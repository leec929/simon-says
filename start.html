Simon Says

by Andy Hong and Steve Lee, CS380

What is Simon Says?

Traditionally, Simon Says is a game where the players follow a given rule when "Simon" gives it.
In this version, Simon Says is a memory game played with 3x3 tiles. Test your memory and see how far you can make it!

Game Play

There are 9 tiles, laid out in a 3x3 fashion. At the beginning of each level, a random tile blinks.
The player must remember the blinking sequence from the first level, and then press the tiles in the correct sequence.
For example, if a green tile blinked in the first level and a blue tile blinked in the second level, the player (to pass level 2) should tap the green tile followed by the blue tile.

Score

  • Your score is equivalent to the number of level you passed.
  • After each game, you can choose to submit your score to the leaderboard.
    • If you take any other action than submitting your score, your current score will be lost.
  • You can see the top 10 scorers on the leaderboard, accessible from the start page.
