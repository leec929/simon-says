Simons Game

by Andy Hong and Steve Lee, CS380


What is Simons Game?

Simons game is a memory game played with 3x3 tiles. Test your memory and see how far you can make it!

Game Play

There are 9 tiles, laid out in a 3x3 fashion. At the beginning of each round, a random tile blinks. The player must remember the blinking pattern in order from the first round, and then press the tiles in the correct seqeunce. For example, if a green tile blinked in the first round and a blue tile blinked in the second round, the player in round 2 should tap the green tile, then the blue tile.

Rules

  • You get two lives per game.
  • Pressing the wrong tile costs a life, and you have to start over in your sequence.
  • Time limit?
  • The game is over when you are out of lives.
  • After finishing a round, you have up to 10 seconds to go on to the next round. After 10 seconds, the next round automatically starts.
  • If you mess up, you get once chance to review the sequence. You must choose this option within 10 seconds before moving on.
